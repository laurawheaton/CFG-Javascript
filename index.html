    Breakfast Burrito

    Ingredients list

      Method

      STEP 1

      Whisk the chipotle paste with the egg and some seasoning in a jug. Heat the oil in a large frying pan & add the kale and tomatoes.

      STEP 2

      Omelet

        STEP 1

                Cheesy Vegetable Pasta Bake

                Ingredients List

                  Method

                  STEP 1

                  Preheat your oven to 220째C/200째C fan/gas mark 7. Halve the pepper and discard the core and seeds. Chop into 2cm chunks. Thinly slice the mushrooms.

                  STEP 2

                  Meanwhile, bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 1/2 tsp salt. When boiling, add the penne to the water and bring back to the boil. Cook until tender, 12 mins. Once cooked, drain in a colander and pop back into the pan. Drizzle with oil and stir through to stop it sticking together.

                  STEP 3

                  While the pasta cooks, trim the courgette, then quarter lengthways. Chop into 2cm chunks. Peel and grate the garlic (or use a garlic press). Halve, peel and chop the onion into small pieces. Heat a large frying pan on high heat (no oil). Once hot, add the courgette and cook until charred, 6-8 mins total. Turn only every couple of mins - this will result in the courgette picking up some nice colour. Once cooked, season with salt and pepper, then transfer to a bowl.

                  STEP 4

                  Return the (now empty) frying pan to medium heat with a drizzle of oil. Once hot, add the onion and pepper and stir-fry until softened, 4-5 mins. Add the mushrooms and fry until starting to brown, 5-6 mins. Add the garlic and stir-fry for 30 secs, then stir in the chopped tomatoes, veg stock paste, tomato puree, Italian style herbs, sugar and water for the sauce (see pantry for both amounts). Bring to the boil, then lower the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened, 7-8 mins.

                  STEP 5

                  While the sauce simmers, grate the Cheddar. Drain the mozzarella and tear into small pieces. Once everything is ready, stir the charred courgette and cooked penne into the sauce. Taste and season with salt and pepper if needed. Transfer to an ovenproof dish and top with the mozzarella and Cheddar. Bake on the top shelf of your oven until the cheese is bubbling and golden brown, 10-15 mins, and serve.

                  Sticky Lemon Tofu

                  Ingredients List

                  Crispy Tofu
                    Lemon Sauce

                      Method

                      STEP 1

                      Drain the tofu and cut it into bite size cubes.

                      STEP 2

                      Place the cubes in a mixing bowl, drizzle the soy sauce over them and stir gently.

                      STEP 3

                      Then add the cornstarch, season with salt and pepper, and stir until the tofu is coated evenly.

                      STEP 4

                      In a large frying pan on medium-high heat, heat up the oil. When it's hot add the coated tofu cubes and fry until they are golden brown. Turn them over using tongs or a spatula to get the tofu evenly fried.

                      STEP 5

                      When the tofu is cooked, transfer it to a plate so you can use the pan to make the sauce.

                      STEP 6

                      Reduce heat to medium. Add more oil to the frying pan, followed by minced garlic and ginger. Fry for 1 minute.

                      STEP 7

                      Then add the water, stock powder, lemon juice, zest, soy sauce and sugar and stir together.

                      STEP 8

                      Finally mix the cornstarch with 2 teaspoons of water, and add this slurry to the pan while stirring. Simmer the sauce until it's thick and sticky. Stir frequently.

                      STEP 9

                      Combine the tofu with the sauce and serve with rice, noodles or veggies of your choice. Garnish with spring onions or sesame seeds.

                      Chicken Alfredo

                      Ingredients List

                        Method

                        STEP 1

                        Season chicken breast with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder from both sides. Add the chicken to a hot grill. If your grill is too hot, you will not need oil and the chicken will not stick to it. Cook the chicken breast for 3 to 4 minutes on each side.

                        STEP 2

                        Remove and cover immediately with another plate or aluminum foil for 3 minutes, this will allow the chicken to cook further from the inside and stay tender. Cut the chicken breast into bite size, and keep aside.

                        STEP 3

                        Boil the spaghetti for nine minutes. Drain and then rinse with cold water.

                        STEP 4

                        In a hot pan, add the butter and the chicken, cook for one minute.

                        STEP 5

                        To make the Alfredo, mix the milk with cornstarch and pour it over the chicken. When it comes to a boil add the Parmesan cheese. Stir for one minute then add the spaghetti. Mix well. If you wish you can season with black pepper.

                        STEP 6

                        Cook for one extra minute and then serve immediately. Sprinkle more Parmesan cheese if you wish.
